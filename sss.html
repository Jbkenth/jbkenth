Our Methodology

The Sip, Snack & Style (SSS) will be set up at the Bachelor of Science in Information Systems Booth Area, ensuring easy access for students, staff, or event-goers. The booth will feature a well-organized layout that separates the food and clothing sections while maintaining a cohesive theme. The location is at the front of the School Director’s Office. All food will be prepared fresh on-site, and the clothing section will feature a carefully curated selection of trendy items that are easy for customers to browse.

Highlights
  • Dual Offering. A perfect mix of street food and fashionable clothing, catering to a wide range of attendees.
  • Affordability. We offer budget-friendly options without compromising on quality, both for food and fashion.
  • Engagement. The interactive booth setup allows customers to enjoy a casual, welcoming atmosphere while shopping and snacking.
  • Convenience. Everything is available in one booth, offering attendees the convenience of grabbing a bite to eat while browsing for trendy outfits.
Our Objectives

The main objectives of Sip, Snack & Style (SSS) for the 62nd Founding Anniversary of SFCG-NOI are to provide a unique dining and shopping experience for event attendees. We aim to deliver high-quality, freshly made street food, such as tempura, fries, hotdogs, and cheese sticks, while offering a selection of trendy clothing at the booth. Our goals include boosting brand awareness, increasing sales during the event, and engaging with a diverse crowd of potential new customers. Additionally, we seek to contribute to the overall success of the Bachelor of Science in Information Systems booth by providing a lively and enjoyable space.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the go-to spot for those who love good food and fresh fashion, creating a community where people can relax, enjoy, and leave feeling satisfied and stylish.

Our Mission

At Sip, Snack & Style, our mission is to provide a fun and welcoming space where people can enjoy delicious street food while exploring trendy and affordable fashion. We’re here to serve up great flavors and great style, all in one place.

About Us

Sip, Snack & Style is all about bringing together two things we love—delicious street food and trendy clothing. We came up with the name because it perfectly captures what we offer. "Sip" represents our refreshing iced tea drinks, "Snack" is all about the tasty street food like tempura, fries, hotdogs, and cheese sticks, and "Style" highlights our passion for fashion, offering a mix of clothing for our customers. We wanted a name that’s simple, catchy, and reflects the fun, laid-back vibe of enjoying great food while staying stylish. We wanted to create a place where people can enjoy the best of both worlds—grabbing a quick snack while browsing through some stylish clothing. The name "Sip, Snack & Style" naturally came to us because it represents exactly what our customers can expect. It’s more than just a shop; it’s a spot where you can relax with your favorite street food, sip on a refreshing drink, and check out trendy clothing options all in one go. We believe that good food and great style can bring people together, and that’s the experience we aim to offer.

Sip, Snack, & Style (SSS) Details

Significance of the Study

  • Significance of the Business
    Sip, Snack & Style (SSS) plays a significant role in enhancing the event experience by combining two popular interests—food and fashion. By offering delicious snacks and trendy clothing at affordable prices, we cater to the practical needs and preferences of attendees. Our presence at the 62nd Founding Anniversary not only serves as a unique attraction but also supports the student community by aligning with the creative and entrepreneurial spirit of the event. The business also helps promote local street food culture and encourages conscious fashion choices.

Scope & Limitation

  • Scope of the Study
    Sip, Snack & Style focuses on providing a unique in-store experience that blends street food with fashionable clothing. Our offerings include a variety of popular snacks like tempura, fries, hotdogs, cheese sticks, and refreshing iced tea, alongside a curated selection of stylish and affordable clothing. We cater to customers seeking convenience, quality, and a welcoming atmosphere. Our business operates solely through our physical store, where customers can enjoy both the food and clothing firsthand.
  • Limitation of the Study
    At Sip, Snack & Style, we’re focused on providing a great in-store experience, which means we currently don’t offer online sales or delivery options. Sometimes, certain items might be out of stock due to supplier issues or high demand, but we always do our best to restock quickly. As a smaller business, we're also limited by space, so while we’d love to expand in the future, right now we’re focused on making our single location the best it can be.

System Used

  • Inventory Management System: Our Inventory Management System helps us keep track of both our food ingredients and clothing stock in real- time. This system ensures that we always have the right supplies on hand, whether it's fresh ingredients for snacks or the latest clothing styles, so we can consistently meet customer demand.
  • Food Safety and Compliance System: We use a Food Safety and Compliance System to ensure that all our food items meet health and safety standards. This system allows us to monitor food preparation, maintain hygiene protocols, and comply with local regulations, guaranteeing that everything we serve is safe and fresh.
  • Financial and Accounting System: Our Financial and Accounting System handles everything from sales tracking to expense management, helping us keep the business running smoothly. It provides insights into our cash flow, manages payroll, and generates financial reports to ensure we stay on top of our finances.
  • Order Management System: Our Order Management System allows us to handle food and clothing orders seamlessly. It helps us track orders from placement to completion, ensuring that every customer receives their snacks and fashion items promptly and accurately, enhancing the overall customer experience.
